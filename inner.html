What is Más que un club ?


FC Barcelona, commonly known as Barça, is more than just a football club. Nestled in the heart of Catalonia's capital, Barcelona, this club represents a unique culture, identity, and history. Globally, Barça is renowned not only for its distinctive style of play and rich heritage but also for its embodiment of Catalan pride and resilience.


The club's motto, "Més que un club" (More than a club), reflects its profound influence beyond the football pitch. Barça has been a symbol of Catalan identity, particularly during periods of political turmoil and repression. Its commitment to promoting Catalan language, traditions, and values underscores its role as a cultural institution.
