FC Barcelona

FC Barcelona, officially known as Futbol Club Barcelona, is a renowned football club based in Barcelona, Catalonia, Spain. Founded in 1899, the club is commonly referred to as Barça. It is famous for its unique membership-based structure and its motto "Mes que un club" (More than a club). Barcelona has a storied history, having won numerous domestic and international titles, including La Liga, Copa del Rey, and the UEFA Champions League. Their home stadium, Camp Nou, is the largest football stadium in Europe. Legendary players such as Lionel Messi, Johan Cruyff, and Ronaldinho have played for the club. Barça is known for its elegant attacking style of play and its excellent youth academy, La Masia.

Very few clubs anywhere in the world have won so many titles. The club's greatest pride and joy remain the five European Cup titles won in Wembley (1992, 2011), Paris (2006), Rome (2009), Berlin (2015) the FIFA Club World Cup (2009, 2011 and 2015).
